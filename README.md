# Hi, I'm Ryan 👋

I'm in the San Francisco Bay Area, moving into product management. I'm not a software developer, but I'm obsessed with finding solutions to problems I notice in my own life. When I spot friction, I build something to fix it. That's how all these projects came about.

### What I'm building

- **Housing History**: an FFXIV plugin that logs every change you make while decorating, with one-click undo. *(Shipped)*
- **Weave**: a personal CRM that nudges you to stay in touch with the people you care about, before you drift apart. *(In development)*
- **Rewind**: a photo cleanup app built around "on this day" batches, so clearing your camera roll feels small instead of overwhelming. *(In development)*
- **Evenly**: a bill splitter that gets you to "who owes what" in a few taps. *(Launching soon)*

### A bit more

By day, I run e-commerce operations for a furniture retailer (pricing, inventory, the whole Shopify storefront). I'm an SJSU MIS grad. The pull toward product really came from a side project: a technology modernization audit I led for a Bay Area nonprofit, benchmarking competitors, prototyping in Figma, and presenting to their executive team. That's the work that made me want to do product full time.

I like small, focused products that solve one problem really well. I've built these projects end to end, which means I've lived the whole arc: spotting the problem, deciding what to build (and what to cut), iterating based on what actually works, and shipping it. That hands-on perspective is what I want to bring to a product role.

I'm currently open to product manager roles.
