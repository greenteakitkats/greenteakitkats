# Hi, I'm Ryan 👋

I'm a product-minded builder in the San Francisco Bay Area, making the move into product management.

Most of what I build starts the same way: I hit a problem in my own life and, instead of living with it, I make the thing that fixes it. Here's what I'm working on right now.

### What I'm building

- **Housing History**: an FFXIV plugin that logs every change you make while decorating, with one-click undo. Built it for my partner. *(Shipped)*
- **Weave**: a personal CRM that nudges you to stay in touch with the people you care about, before you drift apart. *(In development)*
- **Rewind**: a photo cleanup app built around "on this day" batches, so clearing your camera roll feels small instead of overwhelming. *(In development)*
- **Evenly**: a bill splitter that gets you to "who owes what" in a few taps. *(Launching soon)*

### A bit more

I like small, focused products that do one thing really well. I've shipped solo, end to end, so I've done the design, the engineering, and the hard part in the middle: deciding what to cut.

When I'm not building, I'm usually playing Final Fantasy XIV with my partner. Housing History actually came out of that. She wanted a way to undo decorating mistakes, so I made one.
